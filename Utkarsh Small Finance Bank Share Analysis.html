Utkarsh Small Finance Bank Stock Analysis

Utkarsh Small Finance Bank Ltd has emerged as one of the most discussed turnaround banking stocks in India. After facing significant pressure due to microfinance stress during FY26, the bank has shown signs of recovery through improving asset quality, stronger capital adequacy and a strategic shift toward secured lending.

Investors looking for undervalued banking stocks may find Utkarsh Small Finance Bank interesting due to its low valuation, improving NPA profile and long-term growth potential.
