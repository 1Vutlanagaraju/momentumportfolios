Indian Railway Finance Corporation (IRFC): A Complete Analysis

What is IRFC?

  • Established in 1986 as the dedicated financing arm of Indian Railways
  • Borrows funds from financial markets to finance railway assets
  • Assets are leased to Indian Railways under long-term agreements
  • Plays a key role in India’s railway infrastructure expansion

Business Model & Operations

  • Sole operating segment: Leasing and financing of railway assets
  • Primary revenue source: Lease income from Indian Railways
  • Strong sovereign backing ensures low credit risk
  • Predictable and stable cash flows

Financial Snapshot (2025)

  • Revenue: ₹268.9 billion

  • EBITDA Margin: ~100%
  • Total Assets: ~₹4.8 trillion

Strengths & Opportunities
