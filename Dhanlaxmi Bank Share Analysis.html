Dhanlaxmi Bank: Comprehensive Analysis 2025

Overview & History

  • Founded in 1927; headquartered in Thrissur, Kerala
  • Small-sized private sector bank with strong regional presence
  • Geographical mix:
    • Kerala: ~58%
    • Tamil Nadu: ~14%
    • Maharashtra: ~7%
    • Karnataka: ~5%
  • Network: 261 branches, 282 ATMs, 17 Business Correspondents
  • Presence across 14 states and 2 Union Territories (Mar 2025)

Business Growth Highlights (FY 2024–25)

  • Total business grew 14.31% YoY to ₹28,219 crore
  • Deposits increased 12.06% to ₹16,013 crore
  • CASA ratio stable at ~29%
  • Gross advances up 17.40% to ₹12,205 crore
  • Credit–Deposit ratio at 76.22%
  • Gold loan portfolio surged 31.88% to ₹4,447 crore (Q2 FY26)

Financial Performance Snapshot

  • Total Income: ₹1,489 crore (+9.53% YoY)
  • Net Interest Income: ₹483 crore (+5.4% YoY)
  • Operating Profit: ₹95 crore (+37% YoY)
  • Net Profit: ₹66.64 crore (+15% YoY)
  • ROE: ~4.78%
  • ROA: ~0.41%
  • Gross NPA: 2.98% (improved from 4.05%)
  • Net NPA: 0.99%

Capital & Asset Quality

  • Capital Adequacy Ratio (CRAR): 16.12% (Basel III)
  • CRAR improved further to ~17.81% as of Sept 2025
  • Provision Coverage Ratio: ~89%
  • Consistent reduction in NPAs indicates improving asset quality
  • No promoter share pledging

Valuation & Market Performance

  • Market Capitalisation: ~₹1,188 crore (Dec 2025)
  • Current Share Price: ~₹30
  • 52-Week Range: ₹22 – ₹46
  • P/E Ratio: ~17.8x
  • P/B Ratio: ~0.96 (below book value)
  • Intrinsic Value (Median Model): ~₹13.32
  • Stock appears overvalued vs intrinsic estimates
  • 1-Year performance underperformed banking sector (-16% to -29%)

Operational Segments & Loan Mix

  • Retail advances (ex-gold loans) grew 22% to ₹3,093 crore
  • Corporate advances increased 23% to ₹4,447 crore
  • MSME portfolio grew 10% to ₹1,872 crore
  • Housing and vehicle loans show steady traction
  • Gold loans remain a key growth engine (~32% YoY growth)

Risks & Challenges

  • Historically high NPAs, though improving
  • Low ROE and low interest coverage ratio
  • Contingent liabilities of ~₹974 crore
  • 5-year sales growth modest at ~6%
  • Intense competition in private banking sector

Strategic Initiatives & Outlook

  • Strengthening retail and gold loan portfolios
  • Focus on asset quality improvement and capital buffers
  • Expansion of branch network and digital banking services
  • Cost optimisation and margin improvement focus
  • Net Interest Margin around ~3.33%

Conclusion: Balanced Opportunity with Caution

Dhanlaxmi Bank has shown steady improvement in business growth and asset quality, supported by strong regional presence and niche focus on gold loans.

However, valuation concerns, low profitability metrics, and historical asset quality risks warrant caution for new investors.

Investor View: Suitable as a long-term, high-risk banking play with careful monitoring of NPA trends, profitability, and capital adequacy.
