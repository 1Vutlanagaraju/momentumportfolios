My Journey: From a Village Hut to the Stock Market

A story of perseverance, self-learning, failures, resilience, and wealth creation through disciplined investing.

👋 About Me

My name is Nagaraju, and I come from a humble family in Mylavaram, a small village near Vijayawada in Andhra Pradesh.

I am the only son of my parents and have two sisters. We grew up in a small hut with a roof made of palmyra leaves. My father studied only up to 7th standard, and my mother completed 5th standard. Despite financial hardships, they always believed education was the key to a better future and encouraged us to study.

I completed my entire education through government schools and colleges. Even during my school days, I was fascinated by the stock market. However, coming from a poor family, I never had the opportunity or resources to participate in it.

📈 Career & Investment Timeline

Early Career

During my college days, I worked as a Computer Instructor earning just ₹1,000 per month. In 2005, I completed my M.Com and later joined a private college as a Junior Lecturer in Commerce with a salary of ₹5,000 per month.

2007 – Marriage

In 2007, I married a wonderful woman who has been my strongest supporter throughout my journey.

2011 – First Step into the Stock Market

A turning point came when I joined a private company as an Executive – Accounts with an annual package of ₹3 lakh. I relocated to Chennai, where my company opened a salary account along with a Demat account. That simple step unknowingly began my stock market journey.

My First Investment

With only ₹2,000 as capital, I purchased my first stock — NIIT Ltd. At that time, I had no knowledge of technical analysis or investing strategies. I simply bought stocks near their 52-week lows and sold them near their highs.

To my surprise, my first investment generated nearly 50% returns.

Learning Through Experience

Inspired by early success, I continued researching companies and investing in stocks such as Kolte-Patil Developers, where I again achieved approximately 50% returns within six months.

Over time, I developed a passion for swing trading and long-term investing while continuing my full-time career.

⚠ Lessons from Failure

Not every decision was successful.

I invested in stocks that eventually became painful lessons. Some companies became insolvent, while others remained stuck in my portfolio for years.

I also attempted to start an LED bulb manufacturing business. Unfortunately, I was misled by a company that promised machinery, raw materials, and buyback support. I lost nearly ₹35,000 in the process.

That experience taught me one of the most valuable lessons of my life:

"Always verify before trusting."

📉 The Options Trading Mistake

During the COVID-19 lockdown in 2020, I fell into the trap of option trading tip providers who promised extraordinary returns.

Driven by greed and urgency, I followed their advice blindly and lost a significant amount of capital.

These painful experiences taught me the dangers of shortcuts, dependency on others, and the importance of proper risk management.

By the end of 2020, I made a firm decision:

No more option trading tips. No shortcuts. Only disciplined investing and swing trading.

🚀 Discovering Wealth Creators

Over the years, I have identified several high-growth companies that generated significant wealth for investors.

  • Dixon Technologies
  • Data Patterns
  • MTAR Technologies
  • Apollo Micro Systems

These investments reinforced my belief that patience and quality stock selection create wealth over time.

🎯 My Mission Through Momentum Portfolios

To share my learning, experiences, successes, and mistakes with fellow investors so they can make informed decisions and avoid costly errors.

I did not start with wealth, privilege, or professional market training. I started with curiosity, a ₹2,000 investment, and a willingness to learn.

My journey proves that patience, continuous learning, and disciplined investing can transform financial lives.
