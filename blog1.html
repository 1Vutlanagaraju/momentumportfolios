Core Swing Lesson: Managing Option Expiry Volatility

When swing trading Indian equities near the monthly derivatives expiry, standard market noise often amplifies near key strike concentrations. Institutions regularly manipulate underlying share prices to extract maximum value from their written options options. As a rule of thumb, avoid using overly tight trailing stops on volatile Wednesdays and Thursdays during expiry week. Give your trades breathing room by sizing your positions conservatively based on hard structural support lines rather than arbitrary intraday percentage drops.
