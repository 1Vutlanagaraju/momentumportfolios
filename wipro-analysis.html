Fundamental Analysis

Wipro continues to benefit from its diversified global client base, strong cash generation capabilities, and strategic focus on artificial intelligence and consulting-led digital transformation. The company remains financially stable with healthy operating cash flows and shareholder-friendly capital allocation policies.

Positive Factors

  • Strong balance sheet with significant cash reserves.
  • ₹15,000 crore share buyback supporting shareholder value.
  • Growing focus on AI and digital transformation services.
  • Consistent dividend payouts.
  • Global client diversification reduces concentration risk.

Key Risks

  • Weak demand environment in global IT spending.
  • Slower revenue growth compared to larger peers.
  • Pressure on operating margins.
  • Dependence on US and BFSI sector spending.
  • Intense competition from global IT companies.
