📌 Investment Perspective

Astra Microwave Products Limited is one of India's leading defence electronics companies with strong capabilities in radar systems, electronic warfare, communication systems and space technologies. The company operates in high-entry-barrier segments and is strategically positioned to benefit from India's rising defence spending, indigenous manufacturing initiatives and expanding space ecosystem. With a healthy order pipeline, improving profitability and growing opportunities across defence and aerospace sectors, Astra Microwave represents a promising long-term play on India's defence technology growth story.
