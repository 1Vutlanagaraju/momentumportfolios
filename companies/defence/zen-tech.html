📌 Investment Perspective

Zen Technologies Limited is one of India's most promising defence technology companies, operating in niche, high-entry-barrier segments such as military simulators, counter-drone systems and autonomous defence solutions. The company benefits from strong industry tailwinds, increasing defence indigenisation and growing export opportunities. While its long-term growth prospects remain attractive, investors should closely monitor order execution, valuation levels and government procurement timelines.
