📖 About the Company

Data Patterns (India) Limited is one of India's leading indigenous defence and aerospace electronics companies. The company designs, develops and manufactures mission-critical electronic systems for land, air, sea and space platforms.

It is among the few vertically integrated defence electronics companies in India with capabilities spanning product design, engineering, manufacturing, testing and life-cycle support.

The company's strong technological capabilities, high entry barriers and growing order book position it as a long-term beneficiary of India's defence indigenization journey.
