  • E-commerce and quick-commerce channels recorded more than 45% growth, reflecting strong consumer demand.

📌 Investment Perspective

LT Foods Limited has evolved into a global branded food company with strong leadership in premium basmati rice and specialty food products. The company benefits from powerful brands such as Daawat and Royal, an integrated farm-to-fork business model and diversified international operations. Growing demand for premium packaged foods, expansion in organic products and increasing penetration across e-commerce channels provide significant long-term growth opportunities. With strong global distribution capabilities and a focus on value-added products, LT Foods is well positioned to benefit from rising global food consumption and premiumisation trends.
