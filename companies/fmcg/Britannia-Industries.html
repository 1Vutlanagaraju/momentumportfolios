Britannia Industries Limited

India's Leading Food Company Delivering Trusted Biscuits, Dairy Products and Packaged Foods to Millions of Consumers

🏢 Company Overview

  • Company Name: Britannia Industries Limited
  • Sector: Fast Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG)
  • NSE Symbol: BRITANNIA
  • BSE Symbol: 500825
  • Founded: 1892
  • Headquarters: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
  • Business Presence: Biscuits, Dairy Products, Cakes, Breads, Rusks and Packaged Foods

📊 Snapshot Data

  • Market Capitalisation: ₹1.40+ Lakh Crore
  • Share Price: Around ₹5,850
  • Distribution Reach: Over 28 lakh retail outlets
  • Manufacturing Network: 13 company-owned and partner facilities
  • Leading Brands: Good Day, Marie Gold, Tiger, Bourbon, NutriChoice, Milk Bikis and Treat
  • One of India's largest and most profitable food companies

👨‍💼 Key Management

  • Rajneet Singh Kohli – Chief Executive Officer & Executive Director
  • Varun Berry – Executive Vice Chairman & Managing Director
  • Experienced leadership team with strong expertise in FMCG, operations and brand management
  • Strong focus on product innovation and premiumisation strategies

💰 Financial Metrics

  • FY26 Revenue: ~₹18,200 Crore
  • FY26 Net Profit (PAT): ~₹2,300 Crore
  • Operating Margin: ~18%
  • Return on Equity (ROE): ~65%
  • Return on Capital Employed (ROCE): ~85%
  • Dividend Yield: ~1.2%
  • Debt Position: Virtually Debt-Free

🚀 Growth Drivers

  • Strong portfolio of market-leading food brands with high consumer loyalty
  • Increasing demand for packaged foods and convenience products in India
  • Premiumisation and expansion into dairy, croissants and adjacent food categories
  • Deep distribution network reaching urban and rural markets
  • Continuous product innovation and investments in manufacturing capabilities
  • Rising disposable incomes and growing penetration of branded foods

⚠️ Key Risks

  • Volatility in prices of wheat, sugar, edible oil and milk products
  • Intense competition from domestic and multinational FMCG companies
  • Changing consumer preferences and health-conscious consumption trends
  • Regulatory risks related to food safety and packaging requirements
  • Margin pressures arising from raw material inflation
  • Premium valuation limiting margin of safety for investors

📰 Latest Developments

  • Reported healthy volume growth driven by premium biscuits and value-added products.
  • Expanded investments in dairy, croissants and adjacent food categories to diversify revenue streams.
  • Continued strengthening of rural distribution and direct reach initiatives.
  • Focused on innovation-led growth through premium offerings and healthier product portfolios.
  • Maintained strong profitability despite fluctuations in commodity prices.

📌 Investment Perspective

Britannia Industries Limited is one of India's highest-quality FMCG businesses, supported by iconic brands, strong pricing power and consistent cash generation. The company has built a dominant position in the biscuits category while steadily expanding into adjacent food segments. Its asset-light business model, robust distribution network and focus on premiumisation position Britannia well to benefit from rising disposable incomes and increasing consumption of packaged foods in India. For long-term investors seeking stable earnings growth and quality compounding, Britannia remains one of the premier FMCG stocks in India.
