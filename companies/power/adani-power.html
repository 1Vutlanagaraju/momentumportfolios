Adani Power Limited

Adani Power Limited is India's largest private-sector thermal power producer and a key company within the Adani Group. The company operates large-scale power generation assets and supplies electricity to utilities and industrial customers across India.

🚀 Growth Drivers

  • Rising electricity demand driven by industrialisation and urbanisation.
  • Large expansion pipeline through greenfield and brownfield projects.
  • Strong revenue visibility due to long-term power purchase agreements.
  • Strategic acquisition of power assets to increase generation capacity.
  • Operational efficiencies and economies of scale improving profitability.

⚠️ Key Risks

  • Coal price volatility can impact operating margins.
  • Regulatory and environmental policy changes may affect profitability.
  • Merchant power prices remain cyclical and volatile.
  • Power generation business requires significant capital expenditure.
  • Global transition towards renewable energy may impact thermal power demand over the long term.

📰 Latest Developments

  • Secured a 1,600 MW long-term power purchase agreement, strengthening revenue visibility.
  • Expanded generation portfolio through strategic acquisition of power assets.
  • Maintains its position as India's largest private thermal power producer with 18.15 GW operating capacity.
  • Management has outlined plans for significant capacity expansion through organic and inorganic growth initiatives.
