CESC Limited

CESC Limited is one of India's oldest integrated power utilities engaged in power generation, transmission and distribution. The company is the flagship power business of the RP-Sanjiv Goenka Group and supplies electricity to millions of consumers across Kolkata and other regions while expanding its renewable energy portfolio.

🚀 Growth Drivers

  • Stable and regulated electricity distribution business with a large consumer base.
  • Expansion into renewable energy and hybrid power projects.
  • Integrated operations across generation, transmission and distribution.
  • Strong backing from the diversified RP-Sanjiv Goenka Group.
  • Increasing electricity demand from residential, commercial and industrial consumers.

⚠️ Key Risks

  • Changes in electricity tariff regulations may affect profitability.
  • High capital expenditure requirements for renewable expansion projects.
  • Dependence on coal and fuel price fluctuations can impact margins.
  • Execution risks in large renewable and distribution projects.
  • Increasing competition from private and renewable power companies.

📰 Latest Developments

  • Signed power purchase agreements for additional renewable energy capacity.
  • Announced plans to develop large-scale hybrid renewable energy projects.
  • Continues strengthening its integrated power business across generation and distribution.
  • Maintains focus on improving operational efficiency and expanding clean energy initiatives.
