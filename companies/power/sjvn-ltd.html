SJVN Limited

🚀 Growth Drivers

  • Ambitious target to become a 25,000 MW company and expand further through diversified energy projects.
  • Rapid expansion in solar, wind and green hydrogen projects.
  • Strong support from the Government of India and Government of Himachal Pradesh.
  • Diversified project portfolio across multiple states and neighboring countries.
  • Long-term power purchase agreements provide stable revenue visibility.

⚠️ Key Risks

  • Project execution delays may increase costs and affect profitability.
  • Hydropower generation depends significantly on rainfall and water availability.
  • Regulatory changes in tariffs and environmental norms may impact earnings.
  • Increasing competition in renewable energy projects.
  • Transmission bottlenecks can delay commissioning and revenue realization.

📰 Latest Developments

  • Achieved record cumulative power generation milestones at key hydro and solar projects.
  • The 1,000 MW Bikaner Solar Power Project crossed one billion units of electricity generation.
  • Continues expanding renewable and thermal projects across India and Nepal.
  • Declared an interim dividend, reinforcing its commitment towards shareholder returns.
