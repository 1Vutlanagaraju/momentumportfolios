Essential Stock Market Terms

📈 Basic Stock Market Terms

  • Stock: Ownership in a company.
  • Share: A unit of ownership.
  • Equity: Ownership interest in a business.
  • Investor: Person investing for long-term wealth creation.
  • Trader: Person buying and selling frequently.
  • Portfolio: Collection of investments.
  • Market Capitalization: Total company value.
  • Large Cap: Large, stable companies.
  • Mid Cap: Medium-sized growth companies.
  • Small Cap: Smaller companies with higher risk and reward.

📊 Market Terms

  • Bull Market: Rising market.
  • Bear Market: Falling market.
  • Rally: Strong upward movement.
  • Correction: Temporary decline.
  • Crash: Sudden market fall.
  • Recovery: Market rebound.
  • Consolidation: Sideways movement.
  • Breakout: Move above resistance.
  • Breakdown: Move below support.
  • Volatility: Degree of price movement.

💰 Fundamental Analysis Terms

  • Revenue: Company's total income.
  • Net Profit: Earnings after expenses.
  • EPS: Earnings Per Share.
  • P/E Ratio: Price relative to earnings.
  • Book Value: Net asset value.
  • P/B Ratio: Price compared to book value.
  • ROE: Return on Equity.
  • ROCE: Return on Capital Employed.
  • Debt-to-Equity: Financial leverage measure.
  • Dividend: Profit distributed to shareholders.

📉 Technical Analysis Terms

  • Trend: General price direction.
  • Support: Buying zone.
  • Resistance: Selling zone.
  • Volume: Number of shares traded.
  • Moving Average: Average stock price.
  • RSI: Momentum indicator.
  • MACD: Trend indicator.
  • Candlestick: Price representation.
  • Doji: Market indecision candle.
  • Hammer: Bullish reversal candle.

🎯 Trading Terms

  • Intraday Trading: Same-day trading.
  • Swing Trading: Holding for days or weeks.
  • Positional Trading: Holding for months.
  • Entry Price: Buying price.
  • Exit Price: Selling price.
  • Stop Loss: Maximum acceptable loss.
  • Target Price: Expected selling price.
  • Risk-Reward Ratio: Reward versus risk.
  • Leverage: Using borrowed money.
  • Profit Booking: Selling to lock gains.

🚀 Investment Terms

  • Diversification: Spreading investments.
  • Compound Interest: Returns earning returns.
  • Capital Gain: Profit from investment.
  • Value Investing: Buying undervalued stocks.
  • Growth Investing: Investing in growing businesses.
  • Momentum Investing: Investing in strong trends.
  • Multibagger: Stock giving multiple returns.
  • Blue-Chip Stock: Large, financially strong company.
  • ETF: Exchange Traded Fund.
  • Mutual Fund: Professionally managed fund.
