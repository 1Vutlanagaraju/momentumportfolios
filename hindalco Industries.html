🏢 Company Overview

Hindalco Industries is India's leading aluminium and copper producer and one of the world's largest aluminium rolling companies through Novelis. The company benefits from rising demand across electric vehicles, renewable energy, transmission infrastructure, construction, and recycling industries.

📈 Growth Drivers

  • Strong aluminium demand from EV and renewable energy sectors.
  • Growing copper demand from power transmission and data centers.
  • Expansion of downstream aluminium product business.
  • Recovery in Novelis operations and global recycling growth.
  • India's infrastructure and manufacturing expansion.
