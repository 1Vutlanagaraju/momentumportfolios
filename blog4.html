Swing trading continues to remain one of the most preferred trading styles for Indian market participants. Unlike intraday trading, swing trading allows investors and traders to hold positions for a few days to a few weeks while aiming to capture short-term momentum moves.

The objective is simple:

  • Find stocks showing strong momentum
  • Enter near important support or breakout zones
  • Maintain disciplined stop-losses
  • Capture favorable risk-to-reward opportunities

Indian markets recently witnessed volatile but stock-specific action. Banking stocks, defense companies, renewable energy plays, and metals continue attracting trader attention.

Market Overview – Nifty and Bank Nifty Trend

The broader Indian market remains in a stock-specific momentum phase.
