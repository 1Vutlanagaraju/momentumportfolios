IIFL Finance Stock Analysis 2026

IIFL Finance Ltd is one of India's leading NBFCs with a dominant position in Gold Loans, MSME Loans, Home Finance and Secured Lending. Following the removal of RBI restrictions on its gold loan business, the company has reported a strong recovery in profitability, AUM growth and investor confidence.

With Assets Under Management crossing ₹1.08 lakh crore and strong ROE, IIFL Finance continues to be one of the most attractive recovery stories in the Indian NBFC sector.

🏆 Business Analysis

Gold Loans remain the largest contributor to IIFL Finance's business, accounting for nearly half of total AUM. The company has successfully rebuilt momentum after regulatory challenges and continues expanding into MSME loans, home finance and secured lending.

The recent $500 million overseas bond raise demonstrates growing confidence from global investors and strengthens future growth prospects.
