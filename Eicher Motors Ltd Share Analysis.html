Royal Enfield

Royal Enfield remains the global leader in the mid-size motorcycle segment and contributes the majority of Eicher Motors' profitability.

VE Commercial Vehicles (VECV)

Eicher Motors owns a majority stake in VECV, which manufactures trucks, buses, and commercial vehicles through its partnership with Volvo.
